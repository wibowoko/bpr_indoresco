  Conclusion

  1. Business Process Reengineering implementation needs empowerment of the employee and all of the organization member because it requires involvement from all of them.

  2. Company need a better human resources management to handle tasks that are required for empowering employees.

  3. In Business Process Reengineering, support from the top level management is a must without support from the top level management, implementation of Business Process Reengineering will lost the direction.

  4. On this action research, it is obvious that the top level management support is really required. And it is proved that the top level management support makes the Business Process Reengineering Research moving smoothly and correctly.

  5. continuous improvement can be implemented after reengineering the business process. Continuous improvement is conducted to keep and increase the momentum, therefore the result of reengineering does not degradate

  6. This action research has a benefit as a bridge between theory and the practice world with this action research, comparison can be made between theory and the actual condition on the field, especially on the manufacturing company. It can expand the view and knowledge of learner and researcher in the Business Process Reengineering area, and also bridging the gap between theory and practice.
  Recommendation

  1. Followed by the next stage which is implementation.

  2. suggest the company to empower their employees, it will make them ready to conduct Business Process Reengineering on the organization.

  3. This action research can be stated as a preliminary study for other detailed researches. This research can be an independent component for bigger research on Business Process Reengineering topic, especially about its implementation on the manufacturing company

  4. Some other research topics that can be executed such as benchmarking the Business Process Reengineering implementation on the manufacturing companies in Indonesia or to measure the readiness of companies in Indonesia, especially manufacturing companies to conduct Business Process Reengineering.
