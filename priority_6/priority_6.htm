PROCESS PRIORITIZATION

 

        Based on interview with top level management and managers, backup with observation in Indoresco, therefore we conclude that the process that must be prioritize to be reengineered, as follow:

  1. Production

  2. International Order Fulfillment

  3. Proforma Generation - International

  4. Purchasing

  5. National Order Processing

  6. National Order Fulfillment

  7. Follow Up

  8. Warehousing

  9. Order Payment

  10. Prospecting

        However this prioritization is not taking effect in this thesis, because we redesign the all core process in Indoresco. The prioritization is important in reengineering implementation. Because reengineering implementation is conducted step by step, never all in simultaneously
