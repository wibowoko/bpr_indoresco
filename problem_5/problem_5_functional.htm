FUNCTIONAL PROBLEM

 

  1. Management & Administration Problem

  • Overlapping responsibility between employee happen

  • Misunderstanding between employees and created negative work environment

  • The administration is still manual, sometime human error could not be avoided

  • The administration workflow is to much paperworks and much of this is ended in garbage can

  • Communication speed is slow and decision making effort is less responsive
  2. Production Problem

  • Machine has error or breakdown that must be fixed

  • Tight deadline from buyers

  • Order that came in production is above the production capacity

  3. Warehouse Problem

  • To Prepare products that will be delivered is take quite a long time

  • Also the product store in warehouse didn't have systematic procedures

  • Products keep in shelf based on experience of warehouse employee. There will be problem if those employee resign

  • Warehouse control is also very weak
