PROCESS  PROBLEM

 

  1. Decision Making & Managerial  Problem

  • Employees were forced to concentrated on the process rather than the customer

  • Document, such as purchase order must be copied several times and distributed between several section or department in different floor and in separate building

  • The transfer time (back and forth) of this document alone could take significant time

  • A high degree of potential human error could happen in the process
  2. Order Fulfillment Problem (National)

  • Stock is empty (rare to happen) or the particular product is not in production line

  • Delay to deliver goods because the delivery vehicle is limited

  • Delay to deliver goods because the buyer give PO's in a very short time

  • Buyer is having problem with payment credit so the delivery is delayed until the credit is resolve.

  3. Purchasing  Problem

  • Purchasing efforts could be made in different department, there are so many people in Indoresco could order a material
