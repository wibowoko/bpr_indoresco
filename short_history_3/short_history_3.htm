PT INDORESCO PROFILE

 

  • Began when CV. Indores was founded on 1970’s

  • CV Indores became PT Indoresco in 1978

  • Manufacturing dustfree chalkboard under brandname SARJANA

  • In 1980’s, founded subsidiary company named PT. Sarana Widyaguna, which is a trading company that export PT. Indoresco product to other countries.

  • First export is for Binney&Smith Australia, which PT. Indoresco become OEM’s for Binney&Smith’s products

  • 1990, in coorporation with Binney & Smith, manufacturing stationery product for kids  under brandname CRAYOLA

  • PT. Indoresco has three large factory plant in Gunung Putri Industrial Complex, West Java

  • Has manufacturing plan in West Java, East Java and China
